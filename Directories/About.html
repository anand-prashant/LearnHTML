A small talk

I am Anand Prashant, a first semester Computer Science and Engineering student at Lovely Professional University, Punjab.


HTML, as a part of my course taught me many things about web designing and publishing. As execution of my knowledge on HTML — this website is the result of it!

This website is more than just a project for me, it’s a journey. When I started building LearnHTML with just HTML and CSS, I thought it would be simple. But very soon, I realized it was something deeper.


In those four days of creating this website, I learned things that no textbook or theory could have taught me. It wasn’t just about writing <h1>, <h2>, or <div> tags… it was about shaping an idea into something real. And when we talk about “idea”, you’re actually flooded with ideas when you start building something. And the challenging part is to pick that “one idea” which can be actually executed, not just in mind but in the browser too!

If I may compare coding to something easily understood, it would be an artist making a painting. Every time I typed in VS Code, I felt like adding a new brushstroke to a canvas.


I can honestly say those four days were some of the most productive and meaningful of my life. I didn’t just learn to code — I felt it. I felt the excitement, the frustration, the small victories, and the joy of watching an idea turn into a real, working website.


To see the code of this page, you may go to the GitHub repository page where all the codes are written for this website.

When your energy is high and your heart is in it, coding becomes something magical. You don’t just see the code — you see the website in your mind, alive, even before it runs on your screen. And that, to me, is the true beauty of creating.

- Anand Prashant
